TASK: Send a Direct Message to a Friend on Facebook
PLATFORM: Web — facebook.com

Step 1: Open your browser and navigate to facebook.com.
Step 2: Log in to your Facebook account if prompted.
Step 3: Locate the Messenger chat panel or icon:
   - On the main feed, look for the speech-bubble / Messenger icon in the top navigation bar (top-right area).
   - Click it to open the chat pop-up.
Step 4: In the chat pop-up, click "New message" or the pencil icon to start a new conversation.
Step 5: In the "To:" search field, type your friend's name. Select them from the suggestions.
Step 6: Click the message input field at the bottom of the chat window.
Step 7: Type your message.
Step 8: (Optional) Enrich the message:
   - Emoji: click the smiley-face icon on the right side of the input.
   - Sticker: click the sticker icon (folded page).
   - GIF: click the GIF icon.
   - Photo or file: click the image or attachment icon.
   - Voice clip: hold the microphone icon.
Step 9: Press Enter or click the Send button (paper-plane icon) to send the message.
Step 10: Confirm delivery — you will see "Sent" or a read receipt below the message if your friend has message receipts enabled.

ALTERNATIVE — Via Messenger.com:
- Navigate directly to messenger.com for a full-screen messaging experience.
- Follow Steps 4–10 from above.

NOTES:
- If you cannot find someone, ensure you are friends with them or that they have their privacy settings set to allow messages from non-friends.
- To send to a group, select "New group chat" and add multiple recipients in Step 5.
- Disappearing messages can be enabled by clicking the clock icon in the chat header.
