TASK: Book a Hotel on Booking.com
PLATFORM: Web — booking.com

Step 1: Open your browser and navigate to booking.com.
Step 2: Sign in to your Booking.com account (top-right "Sign in") to save the booking and earn Genius discounts.
Step 3: In the main search widget:
   - Destination field: type the city or hotel name.
   - Check-in / Check-out: click each date field and select your travel dates from the calendar.
   - Guests / Rooms: click this field and set the number of adults, children (with ages), and rooms.
Step 4: Click the blue "Search" button.
Step 5: On the results page, apply filters from the left panel:
   - Price range: drag the slider to your budget.
   - Star rating: select the desired star level.
   - Property type: hotel, apartment, hostel, etc.
   - Guest review score: filter for "Very Good" (8+) or higher.
   - Amenities: free WiFi, parking, breakfast included, pool, etc.
Step 6: Sort results by "Top picks for solo travellers", "Price (low to high)", or "Best reviewed".
Step 7: Click a property that interests you.
Step 8: On the property page, review:
   - Photo gallery.
   - Guest reviews and score breakdown.
   - Room type options and what is included (breakfast, cancellation policy, bed type).
Step 9: Select the room type that suits you by clicking "I'll reserve" or "Reserve".
Step 10: On the booking form, fill in:
   - Guest name (as it appears on your ID).
   - Contact email and phone number.
   - Any special requests (high floor, crib, late check-in).
Step 11: Review the price breakdown, taxes, and cancellation policy.
Step 12: Enter payment details (credit card or other accepted methods).
Step 13: Check the box to confirm you agree to the terms.
Step 14: Click "Complete booking".
Step 15: Save the confirmation email and booking reference number.

NOTES:
- Free cancellation rooms cost slightly more but offer flexibility — prefer them when plans may change.
- Genius Level 2/3 unlocks extra discounts; activate by making two qualifying bookings.
