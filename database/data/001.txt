TASK: Buy Headphones on Amazon
PLATFORM: Web — amazon.com

Step 1: Open your browser and navigate to amazon.com.
Step 2: Sign in to your Amazon account (top-right "Hello, sign in").
Step 3: Click the search bar, type "headphones", and press Enter.
Step 4: Use the left-sidebar filters to narrow results:
   - Set a price range matching your budget.
   - Enable "Prime" to filter for fast-shipping items.
   - Set minimum customer rating to 4 stars.
Step 5: Click a product tile that looks promising.
Step 6: On the product page, read the description, specifications tab, and customer reviews section.
Step 7: If the product has variants (color, model tier), select the desired option from the dropdown or button group.
Step 8: Confirm the seller is "Ships from and sold by Amazon.com" for reliability.
Step 9: Click the yellow "Add to Cart" button.
Step 10: In the cart pop-over, click "Proceed to checkout".
Step 11: Verify the shipping address; update if needed.
Step 12: Select a shipping speed (standard or expedited).
Step 13: Review the order summary — quantity, price, tax, shipping cost.
Step 14: Select or enter a payment method.
Step 15: Click the orange "Place your order" button.
Step 16: Copy the order confirmation number displayed on screen for your records.

NOTES:
- If the item is sold by a third-party seller, check their rating (aim for 95 %+ positive).
- Watch for "Frequently bought together" upsells — dismiss if not needed.
- Coupon codes can be applied on the cart page before checkout.
