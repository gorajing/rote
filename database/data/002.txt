TASK: Buy a Vintage Item on eBay
PLATFORM: Web — ebay.com

Step 1: Navigate to ebay.com in your browser.
Step 2: Sign in to your eBay account.
Step 3: Type a description of the vintage item you want in the top search bar (e.g., "vintage Polaroid camera 1970s") and press Enter.
Step 4: On the results page, apply filters:
   - Condition: select "Used" or "For parts or not working" depending on your tolerance.
   - Item Location: set to your country to reduce shipping costs.
   - Listing Type: choose "Buy It Now" to skip auctions, or "Auction" if you want to bid.
Step 5: Sort results by "Best Match" or "Price + Shipping: lowest first".
Step 6: Click a listing that interests you.
Step 7: Carefully read the full item description, paying attention to any mentioned flaws or defects.
Step 8: Zoom in on all provided photos. If photos are insufficient, use the "Ask a question" link to request more from the seller.
Step 9: Check the seller's feedback score; aim for 98 %+ positive feedback with at least 50 reviews.
Step 10: Review the return policy (look for "Returns accepted" and the return window duration).
Step 11: If it is a "Buy It Now" listing, click "Buy It Now". If it is an auction, enter your maximum bid and click "Place bid".
Step 12: On the checkout page, confirm the shipping address.
Step 13: Select a shipping option if multiple are offered.
Step 14: Choose a payment method (PayPal, credit card, eBay managed payments).
Step 15: Click "Confirm and pay" to complete the purchase.
Step 16: Save the order confirmation email and track the shipment via the "My eBay > Purchase history" page.

NOTES:
- For rare items, set a "Saved search" alert so eBay notifies you when a matching listing appears.
- Bid in the last 30 seconds of an auction ("sniping") to avoid driving the price up early.
- eBay's Money Back Guarantee protects buyers if items are not as described.
